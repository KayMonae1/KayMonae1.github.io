Definding Moment English

In my English class this year we worked on something called a Definding Moment. A Definding Moment is something that had a big effect or impact on your life. I chose to do the death of my brother back in 2011. He was killed by the police . I chose to do this moment because this changed me life because I wouldnt be the person I am today if it wasnt for my brother.
